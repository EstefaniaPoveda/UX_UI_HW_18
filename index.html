My UX Skills

User Research

User Empathy

When I create experiences for users it is really important that I take a step back and try to put myself in the user’s position. Making sure my creations are accessible and inclusive to all

Design

My Work

Mobile Resource App for International Students

SomeBuddy is to help international students find others who may be going through the same thing as them. The app also provides resources to help them navigate the city and a helpline in case they need a professional.

Government UI Redesign Analysis

A Canada Post UI redesign was conducted to improve the websites navigation. Specifically the navigation bar which was shown to be difficult to use and overwhelming  for participants when conducting usability tests. 

Non-For Profit UI Redesign Analysis

A Food4Kids Guelph UI redesign was conducted to improve how their goal are conveyed, accessibility issues, design practices and their entire navigation system. It was also important to make sure CTA’s were clear and visible.
